Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

1. No Data Collection

GPS-Photo operates entirely within your browser. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your photos or personal data on external servers.

2. Local Processing

All image processing, including EXIF metadata reading and GPS data writing, is performed locally on your device. Your photos never leave your browser.

3. Storage

The extension uses Chrome's local storage API to save your preferences, selected GPS coordinates, and license information. This data remains on your device.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Gumroad for license verification and payment processing. Please refer to Gumroad's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle your data.

5. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
