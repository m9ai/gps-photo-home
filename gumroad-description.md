# GPS-Photo Pro

**Write GPS location data into your photos — 100% locally in your browser.**

GPS-Photo is a privacy-first Chrome extension that lets you add GPS metadata to JPEG photos without ever uploading them to a server. All processing happens directly in your browser.

---

## What's Included

With this Pro license, you unlock the following premium features:

### Batch Processing
Process up to 50 photos at once with the same GPS coordinates. Perfect for geotagging an entire photo shoot in seconds.

### Saved Location Templates
Save frequently used coordinates as named templates. One click to load your favorite locations — no more retyping coordinates.

### Processing History
Keep track of all your past edits. Quickly reuse previous GPS settings with a single click.

---

## How It Works

1. Open the GPS-Photo extension in Chrome
2. Select one or multiple JPEG photos
3. Pick a location on the interactive map or enter coordinates manually
4. Click **Write GPS & Download** — your updated photo is saved instantly

---

## Why GPS-Photo?

- **100% Private** — Your photos never leave your browser. No server, no upload, no tracking.
- **Local Processing** — All EXIF reading and GPS writing happens on your device.
- **Interactive Map** — Visual location picker with search support (powered by OpenStreetMap).
- **Full GPS Support** — Latitude, longitude, altitude, direction, speed, and timestamp.
