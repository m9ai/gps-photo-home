Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe?

Yes. GPS-Photo processes everything locally in your browser. Your photos are never uploaded to any server.

What photo formats are supported?

Currently JPEG and JPG files are supported. The extension writes standard EXIF GPS metadata that is readable by any photo viewer.

How does the Pro license work?

After purchase on Gumroad, you will receive a license key. Enter it in the extension to activate Pro features instantly.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes. Monthly and Yearly subscriptions can be cancelled at any time from your Gumroad account. You will continue to have Pro access until the end of your billing period.

Does the Lifetime license include future updates?

Yes. The Lifetime license includes all current and future Pro features with no additional cost.
